Our story

Two women. One shared table.

Born from tradition,
made for the neighbourhood.

Founded by two women from Greece, Pavillion brings the warmth of Mediterranean baking to Toronto. Every flaky layer and aromatic dessert is made with care, quality ingredients, and respect for the craft.

Greek family recipes meet Canadian favourites and flavours from around the world—a menu that feels just like the Danforth: familiar, generous, and wonderfully diverse.

“Food is more than flavour—
it’s connection.”
